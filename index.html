03 — About the Artist

Nadeen Rouphael

Nadeen Rouphael is a distinguished interior designer and artist based in Beirut, Lebanon. As the founder of Atelier Nadeen, she has carved a niche for herself in the realms of both interior design and ceramic art. Her creative journey began with a deep-rooted passion for art, which she completes through her work with paint and ceramics. With extensive experience working alongside European designers in the furniture industry, Nadeen developed a profound interest in ceramic art. This newfound passion allowed her to explore and present unique perspectives, offering fresh and innovative angles on design. Her work is a testament to her ability to blend artistic expression with functional aesthetics.
