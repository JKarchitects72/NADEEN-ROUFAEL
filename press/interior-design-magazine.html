Editorial Feature

Interior Design Magazine

In its 10 Questions With… series, Interior Design magazine speaks with Nadeen Rouphael about her path from architecture and interiors into ceramics, and the founding of her studio, Atelier Nadeen.

She describes how ceramics began as a hobby and grew into a defining practice, her years working alongside European furniture designers, and her instinct to challenge conventional design through colour, texture and form.

It is an approach that has carried her work from the studio to international stages, including Dubai Design Week and the MENART Fair in Brussels.
