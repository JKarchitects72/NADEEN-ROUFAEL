“Recognized by AD Magazine as one of the most remarkable ceramists of our time, acknowledging her significant contributions to the field.”

In its feature The Most Remarkable Women Ceramic Artists Right Now, AD Middle East spotlights Nadeen Rouphael among the leading women shaping ceramics across the region.

The piece celebrates the bold colour, tactile surfaces and playful, almost organic sense of movement that define her sculptural forms — and the distinctive vision behind Atelier Nadeen.
