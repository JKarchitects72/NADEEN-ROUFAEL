Art of Living — Abu Dhabi

Versatile

Versatile /ˈvəːsətʌɪl/ adj. — able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities.

Versatile is an ensemble of intricately designed pieces built with a multitude of materials that flourish into a bouquet of shapes, colours and sounds which you experience by entering its space.

Kōzō is a wooden structure made for you to create with. Whether it’s an artistic piece that merges colour and light or a bookshelf in your living space, the expansive flexibility it offers will let you turn it into one of a kind.

Kumo is a wooden structure made for you to create with. Whether it’s a bench with multiple random legs that form a solid unit — a module with a completely customisable shape — on top of which sit three or more circular poufs, or a glass surface that renders it as a table, the expansive flexibility it offers will let you turn it into one of a kind.

The sculptures featured represent a collection of a daring work of colours. The created shapes turn the fixed pieces into life, offering them a sense of movement almost to the likes of plants. Any angle will speak its own truth and convey its own thoughts.

Shapes and colours wouldn’t do justice to the uniqueness of each piece, and that is why a third physical trait presents itself: texture.

This is Atelier Nadeen × JK Architects — this is Versatile — this is The Art of Living.
